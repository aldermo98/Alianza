/ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions .

Get answers to common questions about our dance classes, pricing, drop-ins, and more. We welcome dancers of all shapes, sizes, and experience levels!

Do I need to have dance experience to join?

No experience is needed! Our classes are designed for all levels, from beginners to advanced dancers. Whether you’ve never danced before or you’re looking to refine your moves, we’ve got you covered.

Are your classes suitable for people of all shapes and sizes?

Absolutely! Our classes are inclusive and designed for people of all shapes, sizes, and fitness levels. Salsa is for everyone, and we celebrate diversity on the dance floor.

Can I drop into a class without a reservation?

Yes, you can! We offer flexible drop-in classes so you can join whenever it fits your schedule. Simply show up and dance!

What types of classes do you offer?

We offer a variety of salsa and Latin dance classes, including beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels. In addition to salsa, we also offer Bachata, Latin rhythm, and private lessons. There’s something for everyone!

How do I book a class?

You can book a class through our website by clicking on any "BOOK NOW" button. It will redirect you to the contact form, at the bottom of this page, where you can send us a message about a specific date you'd like to stop in! Feel free to drop in, too, no booking required!
