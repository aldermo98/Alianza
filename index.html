Meet the Instructors .

Amanda and Eddie are the passionate instructors behind Alianza Salsa Dance Company. With years of experience in salsa dancing, they bring energy, expertise, and a love for dance to every class. Amanda's vibrant teaching style and Eddie's technical precision create a dynamic learning environment where students of all levels can thrive. Their commitment to fostering a supportive and fun atmosphere ensures that everyone feels welcome and motivated to unleash their inner dancer.

What to expect.

No partner? No problem! In our salsa classes, you don’t need to bring a dance partner because our instructors rotate and dance with everyone. This means you’ll get personal guidance every step of the way, making it easy to learn, improve, and have fun—without any pressure. Whether you're a total beginner or just brushing up on your moves, you'll always have an experienced dancer to help you find the rhythm and feel confident on the dance floor. Just show up, dance, and enjoy the music!

Frequently Asked Questions .

Get answers to common questions about our dance classes, pricing, drop-ins, and more. We welcome dancers of all shapes, sizes, and experience levels!

Do I need to have dance experience to join?

No experience is needed! Our classes are designed for all levels, from beginners to intermediate dancers. Whether you’ve never danced before or you’re looking to refine your moves, we’ve got you covered.

Are your classes suitable for people of all shapes and sizes?

Absolutely! Our classes are inclusive and designed for people of all shapes, sizes, and fitness levels. Salsa is for everyone, and we celebrate diversity on the dance floor.

Can I drop into a class without a reservation?

Yes, you can! We offer flexible drop-in classes so you can join whenever it fits your schedule. Simply show up and dance!

What types of classes do you offer?

We offer salsa on 2 dance classes for beginner and intermediate levels on Tuesdays and Thursdays, respectively.

How do I book a class?

We offer dropins! Feel free to stop on by or send us a message through our web form at the bottom of this page.
