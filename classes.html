Salsa Class Details

Basic Step Breakdown & Body Movement

Learn the foundation of Salsa On2 with a focus on mastering the basic steps, improving rhythm, and adding natural body movement. Perfect for beginners or dancers looking to strengthen their fundamentals.

Spinning Technique, Shines & Partnerwork

Develop clean, balanced spins and dynamic shines while enhancing your partner connection. This class blends solo footwork with lead–follow techniques for a well-rounded skill set.

Intro to Salsa On2

Start your Salsa On2 journey with this welcoming introductory class. You’ll learn timing, step patterns, and essential techniques to dance confidently in social settings.

Beginner On2 Shines & Partnerwork

Build your confidence with easy-to-follow shines and beginner-friendly partnerwork. This class bridges the gap between fundamentals and more complex combinations.

Rotating Specialty Class – Social Dancing Turn Patterns

Each week features new turn patterns and combinations designed to make your social dancing smooth, stylish, and fun. Great for adding variety to your repertoire.

Intermediate On2 Shines & Partnerwork

Challenge yourself with faster-paced shines, intricate footwork, and more advanced partner combinations. Ideal for dancers ready to level up their On2 skills.
