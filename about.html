Meet Alianza Dance Company

Welcome to Alianza Dance Company, your home for salsa lessons in Sacramento! We've been dancing, teaching, and building community through salsa for over 10 years. Whether you're brand new to the dance floor or looking to take your skills to the next level, our studio offers a fun, supportive space to grow and connect.

Our Journey

Alianza was born from love—both for dance and for people. Founders Eddie and Amanda started the company after falling in love with salsa and realizing the impact it had on their lives. What started as informal dance nights among friends in Sacramento quickly evolved into packed classes, local performances, and a thriving salsa family.

We've been teaching salsa in Sacramento for over a decade, helping hundreds of students find confidence, rhythm, and joy on the dance floor. From social salsa nights to professional choreography, we've seen firsthand how salsa transforms people—physically, emotionally, and socially.

Who We Are

Eddie brings deep musical knowledge and cultural authenticity to every class, with roots in traditional rhythms from his home country of Peru. His teaching is grounded in musicality, history, and connection to the essence of Latin dance. Amanda is the powerhouse of style and precision—known for her signature flair, sharp technique, and mastery of salsa footwork. Together, they create the perfect balance of rhythm and expression in every lesson.

Together, they’ve created a welcoming and empowering environment where everyone feels at home—no matter their age, experience, or fitness level.

What We Offer

We specialize in Salsa-on-2 with a variety of Sacramento salsa classes including:

  • Beginner salsa lessons in Sacramento
  • Intermediate level salsa technique
  • Private salsa lessons
  • Couples salsa classes for date nights or weddings
  • Performance training and choreography
Our teaching style is rooted in fun, clarity, and connection. We don’t just teach you steps—we help you become a dancer.

Why Choose Alianza?

There are plenty of places to take salsa classes in Sacramento, but what sets us apart is our commitment to personal growth, cultural authenticity, and community. When you dance with Alianza, you're not just learning moves—you're joining a movement.

Come experience the rhythm, energy, and connection of salsa. We can’t wait to meet you on the dance floor!
